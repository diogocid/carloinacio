About

Carlo graduated in Art and Design at Escola Superior de Educação de Coimbra, in 2009. During his education he was a apprentice industrial designer at the company Sit Urban Design in Leiria. Participated in the ERASMUS program for a semester and studied Three Dimensional Design at the University of Portsmouth in England. At the end of his degree he lived and worked for two years as lead designer at Ruy Rezende Architecture, in Rio de Janeiro - Brazil. In 2012, he returned to Portugal and formed the company CIDI - Carlo Inácio Design Industrial, with the main areas of intervention, being Three-Dimensional Images, Interior Design, Product Design and Graphic design.
